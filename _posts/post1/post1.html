Daily Cases

Based on a report from Anadolu Agency Karaalp (2020) the first case in Iraq occurred on 24 February. After that the second case was reported two days later with a jump of 4 cases at once. Entering March, daily cases tended to fluctuate. However, the cumulative cases increased significantly. The first case of death due to Covid-19 was reported on March 4, as many as 2 cases. Until the last data is obtained on the date 2020-08-31 a total of - positive cases, - died, and NAdeclared cured.
